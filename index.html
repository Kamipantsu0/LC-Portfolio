Know more about AIESEC
AIESEC is the world’s largest youth-led organization, present in over 100 countries and territories. Since 1948, we have empowered young people to become value-driven leaders through international exchanges, real-world experiences, and impactful projects. By connecting with diverse cultures and taking initiative, our members grow into responsible and globally-minded changemakers.

AIESEC in Tunisia
Every great organization is built upon strong local foundations. Within AIESEC in Tunisia, our local committees bring our global mission to life at the community level. LC Medina proudly represents the student community of Manouba University serving as a hub for leadership development, innovation, and impactful projects. Rooted in the cultural and academic spirit of Manouba, we are not just an organization; we are a dynamic family dedicated to nurturing future leaders and fostering meaningful change on our campus and beyond.


Leading with purpose since 1989, LC Medina continues to shape generations of young change makers.
